Common questions

What items do you accept?
We accept most second-hand furniture in reasonable condition — sofas, chairs, tables, desks, shelves, and more. Heavily damaged items may not be accepted after evaluation.
How is the pickup fee calculated?
The pickup fee covers transport and evaluation. It's a fixed cost deducted from your payout once your item sells. If an item doesn't sell, you won't owe anything extra.
When do I get paid?
Payouts are processed once a buyer completes their order. You'll get a notification when your item sells and again when the payout is on its way.
Can I cancel a pickup request?
Yes — you can cancel before our team has collected your items. Once collected, the evaluation process begins and cancellation is no longer possible.
